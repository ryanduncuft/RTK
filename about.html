Early Life and Musical Beginnings

Born and raised in the Greater Manchester town of Mossley, Ryan William Duncuft embarked on his musical journey in 2019. Drawing inspiration from a diverse palette of genres, including rock, alternative, hip hop, and trap, he began experimenting with his sound at a young age. His early work was a testament to his raw talent, recorded using little more than a cell phone and basic earphones on the BandLab app. Over time, he honed his production skills using FL Studio, a process that allowed him to self-produce a significant portion of his discography and solidify his unique sonic identity.

Career and Artistic Evolution

Ryze Tha Kidd quickly made a name for himself in the UK music scene with his innovative, genre-blending sound. His music often serves as a candid reflection of his personal experiences, resonating with a growing audience. A defining element of his artistry is his embrace of the emo rap genre, a style he delivers with raw authenticity and melodic complexity. As a fiercely independent artist, he continues to push the boundaries of his sound, releasing impactful projects through his own label, Ryze Collective Media Group.

Filmography & Business Ventures

Demonstrating a vision that extends far beyond music, Ryze Tha Kidd has actively expanded his creative and business portfolio. He is the founder of Ryze VFX Film, a production company dedicated to visual storytelling. In a further move to connect directly with his audience, he is developing RTK+, a comprehensive streaming platform. This platform is set to become the exclusive home for his official music videos, behind-the-scenes content, and future film projects, showcasing his multifaceted artistic and entrepreneurial ambitions.
